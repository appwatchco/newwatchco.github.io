Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: MindfulFlow does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All data the app generates stays on your device. We do not run any servers, analytics, or advertising.

1. Who We Are

New Watch Co Limited is the data controller for any personal data processed in connection with our apps and this website. We are registered in England and Wales.

Contact: james@newwatchco.com

2. Data We Collect

In the MindfulFlow app:

We do not collect any personal data. All data generated by the app — including your screen time statistics, pickup counts, streak progress, and app shield settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device frameworks. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

On this website (newwatchco.com):

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind. The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may process your IP address solely to serve the page — see GitHub's Privacy Statement. The site loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address — see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (UK & EU GDPR)

Where we process any personal data, we rely on the following legal bases under UK GDPR and EU GDPR:

  • Legitimate interests — to provide, maintain, and improve our apps and website.
  • Contract performance — to fulfil purchases made through the App Store.
  • Legal obligation — where required to comply with applicable law.

Because MindfulFlow processes all data on-device, there is no server-side personal data processing by New Watch Co Limited.

4. Apple and Third-Party Frameworks

MindfulFlow uses Apple's Screen Time, Family Controls, and StoreKit frameworks. Data processed by these frameworks is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Apple handles all payment processing; we never receive your card details or billing information.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Data stored on your device by the app is controlled by you and can be removed at any time by deleting the app.

6. Your Rights Under UK & EU GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and EU GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — to request that we restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making — we do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at james@newwatchco.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Right to Complain

If you are based in the UK and believe we have not handled your personal data in accordance with UK GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
Phone: 0303 123 1113

If you are based in the EU, you may contact your local Data Protection Authority.

8. International Transfers

We do not transfer personal data outside the UK or European Economic Area. All app data is processed on-device. Our website is hosted on GitHub's servers (US-based); GitHub maintains Standard Contractual Clauses for EU/UK data transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated 17+ on the App Store and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If Family Controls features are used, this requires authorisation from a parent or legal guardian.

10. Changes to This Policy
